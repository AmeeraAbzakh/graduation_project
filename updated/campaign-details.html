About This Campaign

On March 14th, our volunteers found 30 stray dogs near the Al-Hashmi district in Amman. Several were injured and malnourished and needed urgent vet care.

We rushed to provide first aid, but full treatment, vaccinations, and temporary shelter cost more than we can cover alone. We're asking the community to help.

Your donation goes toward: emergency vet care, anti-parasite treatment, food for 60 days, and kennel space while we find them homes.

We've already partnered with two Amman vet clinics at reduced rates. With your help, we can save all 30 dogs before the end of the month.
